# HôtelManager Pro — Product Paper
### The All-in-One Hotel Management Platform for African & Emerging-Market Hotels

---

## What is HôtelManager Pro?

**HôtelManager Pro** is a complete, cloud-based hotel management system built for independent hotels, small chains, and hospitality groups. It replaces paper logs, spreadsheets, and disconnected tools with a single platform that connects every department — Reception, Restaurant, Housekeeping, Accounting, and Management — in real time.

Designed from the ground up for the realities of West and Central African hotel operations, it works in **French and English**, handles **FCFA currency** natively, and is resilient enough to operate through unstable internet connections.

## System Modules

---

### 1. 🏠 Reception & Guest Management

The heart of daily operations. Everything that happens at your front desk is handled here.

**Features:**
- Full **guest registration** (name, nationality, ID/passport number, date of birth, phone)
- **Check-in wizard** with room assignment, number of nights, negotiated price, and payment mode
- **Arrangement / negotiated pricing** — override the standard rate and track it accurately throughout the stay
- **Active stays dashboard** — see every guest currently in the hotel, their room, days elapsed, and outstanding balance
- **Check-out flow** with final invoice review, balance settlement, multiple payment methods (cash, card, mobile money, transfer)
- **Guest payment history** per stay
- **Reservation calendar** — visual grid showing room availability by date
- **Reservations management** — create advance bookings linked to guest profiles

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Check-ins, check-outs, occupancy rate, pending balances |
| Weekly | Occupancy trend, revenue per room type, average stay length |
| Monthly | Total stays, total nights sold, average occupancy, RevPAR |

---

### 2. 😴 Siestes (Day-Use / Short Stays)

A unique feature built for hotels that offer daytime room use — a common and profitable service in Central and West Africa.

**Features:**
- Register a guest for a **daytime rest** with configurable default duration (e.g. 3 hours)
- Auto-calculate **overtime charges** if the guest stays beyond the allocated time
- Link sieste to an existing guest profile or create a walk-in
- Issue invoices and accept payment at point of use
- Full daily sieste log per room

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Siestes recorded, total sieste revenue, overtime charges |
| Weekly | Most-used sieste rooms, peak sieste hours |
| Monthly | Sieste revenue share of total room revenue |

---

### 3. 🍽️ Restaurant & Kitchen

Complete front-of-house and back-of-house restaurant management, linked directly to rooms and guest invoices.

**Features:**
- **Digital menu management** — categories, items, prices, descriptions
- **Waiter order entry** — select room or table, add items, send to kitchen instantly
- **Kitchen Display System (KDS)** — orders appear live on a kitchen screen; staff mark dishes as in preparation and done
- **Charge to room** — restaurant orders can be added directly to a guest's hotel invoice
- **QR code menus** — generate printable QR codes per room that guests scan to order from their phone, with no app download required
- **Order status tracking** — pending, in preparation, served
- **Restaurant revenue reports** — daily sales, best-selling items, average ticket value

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Orders count, total restaurant revenue, top dishes, average ticket |
| Weekly | Revenue by service (breakfast / lunch / dinner), wastage tracking |
| Monthly | Menu performance, category breakdown, revenue vs. prior month |

---

### 4. 🏠 Rooms & Categories

Manage your physical inventory of rooms and their configuration.

**Features:**
- Define **room categories** (Standard, Superior, Suite, etc.) with base price per night
- Create individual **room records** with room number and type
- Real-time **room status board**: Available, Occupied, Dirty, Under Maintenance
- Status auto-updates on check-in/check-out
- Occupancy reconciliation against active stays

---

### 5. 📋 Main Courante (Daily Hotel Ledger)

The **Main Courante** is the cornerstone of professional hotel accounting in French-speaking Africa. It is the official daily summary of all financial movements in the hotel.

**Features:**
- Auto-populated every morning for the current date
- One row per active guest/room — tracks: room charges, restaurant charges, bar/minibar, services, payments, and outstanding balance
- Manual entry capability for walk-in or exceptional charges
- Day-by-day navigation (previous and future dates)
- **Lock/close** a day once verified to prevent retroactive changes
- Inline payment recording per client row
- Running totals: **Total Revenue (CA)**, **Cash Collected (Encaissement)**
- Negotiated price propagated correctly from check-in data

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Full ledger with per-guest breakdown, CA vs. encaissement |
| Weekly | Revenue trend, uncollected balances by guest |
| Monthly | Net revenue, reconciliation with invoices and cash sessions |

---

### 6. 💰 Cash Register & Expenses

End-to-end cash management for front-desk cashiers and accountants.

**Features:**
- **Cash session management** — open a session at the start of each shift, record all movements during the session, close it with a physical count and reconciliation
- **Balance tracking** — opening balance, entries, exits, expected closing balance vs. physical count
- **Cash difference alert** — instantly shows shortfall or excess at session close
- **Expense recording** — log any operational expense (supplies, maintenance, staff costs, etc.) with category, amount, and date
- **Expense categories** — fully customizable
- **Approval workflow** — expenses can be submitted and approved by managers
- **Payment methods** — cash, card, mobile money, bank transfer

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Cash session summary, total in/out, cash difference |
| Weekly | Expense breakdown by category, cash flow summary |
| Monthly | Total expenses vs. revenue, approved vs. pending expenses, profit margin |

---

### 7. 📦 Inventory Management

Track and control all physical stock in the hotel — from minibar items to cleaning supplies.

**Features:**
- Add inventory items with buying price, selling price, unit, current stock, and minimum stock level
- **Minibar flag** — mark items for minibar tracking on rooms
- **Low stock alerts** — automated notifications when stock falls below minimum
- Stock movements automatically recorded when items are used (e.g. restaurant orders consume inventory)
- Manual stock adjustment for deliveries, losses, and audits
- Buying vs. selling price tracking for margin analysis

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Items consumed, current stock levels, low-stock alerts |
| Weekly | Fast-moving vs. slow-moving items, stock value |
| Monthly | Stock turnover, total value consumed, reorder recommendations |

- One-click status updates — staff tap a button to move their room to the next stage
- Supervisor inspection step before marking a room as clean
- **Real-time updates** — changes appear instantly for all users without refreshing
- Automatic task creation after check-out

**Reports available:**
| Period | Data |
|---|---|
| Daily | Rooms cleaned, avg. cleaning time, pending inspections |
| Weekly | Staff performance, rooms cleaned per person |
| Monthly | Cleaning cycle analytics, turnaround time by room type |

### 13. 🔐 User Roles & Access Control

Granular permissions so every employee sees only what they need to.

| Role | Module Access |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access to all modules |
| Manager | Full access except super-admin tools |
| Receptionist | Reception, Check-in/out, Guests, Siestes, Main Courante, Inventory |
| Accountant | Cash & Expenses, Reports, Main Courante, Billing |
| Restaurant | Restaurant orders and menu |
| Kitchen | Kitchen Display only |
| Housekeeping | Housekeeping task board only |

- **Disable accounts** without deleting them
- **Promote to owner** — flag responsible owner-level users per property
- Role-based page protection — unauthorized users see an access-denied screen

### 15. 🏢 Multi-Hotel Management (Groups & Owners)

Built for hotel groups and investors who own or manage more than one property.

**Features:**
- **One account, multiple hotels** — switch between properties from the header in one click
- Role and permissions are stored per hotel — a user can be Admin at one hotel and Receptionist at another
- **Super Admin dashboard** — view and manage all hotels from a single administrative screen
- Create new hotels, configure plans, enable/disable properties
- Assign team members to hotels and set their role
- Generate invitation links to onboard new staff to a specific hotel

### 16. 📜 Audit Log

Full traceability for compliance and fraud prevention.

**Features:**
- Every significant action in the system is logged: who did it, when, and what data changed
- Audit log includes: invoice creation, payment recording, check-in/check-out, expense approval, stock adjustment
- Accessible only to admins and managers
- Non-deletable log entries — immutable record

## Technology & Reliability

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Platform | Web (any browser), Windows Desktop App |
| Cloud backend | Supabase (PostgreSQL, real-time, Row Level Security) |
| Offline resilience | Critical operations queue locally and sync when connection returns |
| Real-time sync | Housekeeping, kitchen orders, and table data update live across all devices |
| Security | Per-hotel data isolation at database level (RLS), encrypted credentials, JWT sessions |
| Data isolation | Each hotel's data is completely separated — staff at Hotel A cannot see Hotel B's data |
